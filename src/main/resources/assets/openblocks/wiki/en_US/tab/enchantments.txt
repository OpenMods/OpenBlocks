[prefix{l}]Last Stand Enchantment [prefix{}]

The last stand enchantment uses your XP bar as a final protection against dying.

When you reach 0.5 hearts, the game will take XP from you whenever you're damaged instead of killing you. The higher number of enchantments you have the cheaper the XP cost will be.
